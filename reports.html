Reports#

Reports are a great way to document how a patch ROM is configured when it is in storage or being shipped out.

Reports contain two sections: a map of circuit numbers to addresses (similar to what is shown in the Editor) and a visual representation of the rack with each address shown in its place.

Reports can be generated in the Browser by right-clicking on a ROM file and choosing Create Report or in the Editor from the Edit menu.
